Life

My journey since since 1992 until today

1992

Born

Born in a very rural and isolated part of Portugal

1999

First contacts with Electronics

At age 9 I started to tinker with electronic devices and fix toys that broke, simple stuff such as broken wires or leaked capacitors. This was purely because my mom showed me how.

2001

First robot cybot

A weekly-issue robotics kit came out in the market, I only bought the first 16 weeks due to lack of money, but this assured basic functionality. It was my first time controlling a machine via sensors, and a good introduction to robotics.

Between this and 2007 I deepened my electronics theory knowledge via magazines and books, I had no internet access at this point.

2002

Ilness and nearly died

Due to complications in getting adequate treatment, a simple appendicitis turned into an acute appendicitis with peritoneal abscess. According to the surgeon I was half an hour away from death. This really got in the way of my projects.

2007

Internet and first time coding
