Startup Founder Assessment

You're considering joining a startup and you're not sure where the founder(s) are in the Visionary <> Clueless spectrum. This resource aims to help you think about the right things to help you assess your particular situation.

With example questions you can ask them, and common things true visionaries say versus things the truly clueless say.

  • help_outlineSample Questions to Ask

    Here are some example questions you could ask the founder to gain insight related to the questions from the questionnaire:

    • Track Record:
      • Can you share the history of your past ventures and their outcomes?
      • What were some of the key lessons you learned from these experiences?
    • Details and Understanding:
      • Can you walk me through your business model?
      • How does your solution address the problem you've identified?
      • Can you describe your target market and key competitors?
    • Flexibility and Openness to Feedback:
      • Can you share an example where you received critical feedback and how you handled it?
      • How open are you to pivot your business model if necessary?
    • Transparency:
      • Can you talk about the biggest challenges your startup is currently facing?
      • How do you plan to overcome them?
      • Can you explain how you manage financial resources and investor relations?
      • Can I interview your top customers?
    • Passion vs Obsession:
      • What drives your passion for this project?
      • How do you maintain a work-life balance while running a startup?
      • Can you provide an example of a time when you had to make a difficult decision for the benefit of your startup, even if it was personally hard for you?
    • Honesty about Risks and Challenges:
      • What are the potential risks your startup might face?
      • How are you planning to mitigate these risks?
      • Can you describe the most significant challenges you foresee for your business?
    • Team and Advisors:
      • Who are the key members of your team, and what are their backgrounds?
      • How many team members have left? What made them leave?
      • How do you select your advisors?
      • Can you share an instance where your team or advisors made a significant contribution to your startup?
      • Do you have a board?

    Remember, the responses to these questions should be evaluated critically. Where possible, verify the information provided independently. Founders who are transparent, honest, and open to feedback are often more reliable and trustworthy.
  • chat_bubble_outlinePotential Responses

    Here's an example of potential responses for each category. However, please note that these are just illustrative and real-life responses can vary greatly based on context and the individual's communication style.

    • Track Record:
      • Visionary: "I previously co-founded a software startup that was acquired by XYZ Corporation after we demonstrated substantial user growth and product-market fit."
      • Clueless: "I've had a couple of business ideas before, none of them really took off but this one is different."
    • Details and Understanding:
      • Visionary: "We're creating a unique value proposition by combining AI and blockchain to disrupt the supply chain industry. Here are the steps we're going to take..."
      • Clueless: "We're doing something with AI and blockchain. I'm not sure about the specifics but it's going to be huge."
    • Flexibility and Openness to Feedback:
      • Visionary: "We have a clear plan but we're prepared to pivot based on market feedback and new opportunities. In fact, we already did so once when..."
      • Clueless: "Our plan is perfect. We don't see any reason to change it."
    • Transparency:
      • Visionary: "We're facing some challenges around customer acquisition and regulatory compliance, but here's how we're addressing them..."
      • Clueless: "Everything's great, we don't have any problems."
    • Passion vs Obsession:
      • Visionary: "I love what I'm doing and I'm committed to making this work, but I also understand the importance of maintaining a balanced perspective and taking care of my team."
      • Clueless: "I work 24/7 and expect my team to do the same. We're all in this 100%, there's no room for slackers."
    • Honesty about Risks and Challenges:
      • Visionary: "While we believe in our vision and potential, we are mindful of certain risks such as..."
      • Clueless: "There are no real challenges or risks. I'm confident this is a billion-dollar idea."
    • Team and Advisors:
      • Visionary: "We have a diverse team with complementary skills, and we're advised by industry veterans. Here's how they've contributed so far..."
      • Clueless: "We don't have a full team yet, but my cousin's going to help out when he finishes school. And my friend's dad knows a lot about business, so we're covered there."
